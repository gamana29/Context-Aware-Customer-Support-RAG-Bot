Riva Customer Support Knowledge Base

Welcome to Riva!

Thank you for choosing Riva. We are dedicated to providing customers with a seamless shopping experience, high-quality products, secure payments, and reliable customer support. This knowledge base contains answers to the most frequently asked questions regarding refunds, deliveries, memberships, account management, support services, and more.

------------------------------------------------------------
1. Refund Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Customers may request a refund within 7 days of receiving their order. The product must be unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging along with all accessories and the original invoice.

Refunds are not available for:
• Gift cards
• Digital products
• Personalized or customized items
• Products damaged due to customer misuse

Once the returned product reaches our warehouse, our quality team inspects it within 2 business days. If approved, the refund is processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

If the refund is delayed, customers should contact our support team with their order number.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. Delivery Timelines
------------------------------------------------------------

Riva offers two delivery options.

Standard Delivery:
• Delivery within 3–7 business days.

Express Delivery:
• Delivery within 1–2 business days for eligible locations.

Customers receive an email and SMS containing the shipment tracking number immediately after dispatch.

Delivery may be delayed due to:
• Bad weather
• Natural disasters
• Public holidays
• High order volumes during festivals

Shipping charges vary depending on location unless covered by membership benefits.

4. Account Cancellation
------------------------------------------------------------

Customers may permanently cancel their Riva account from the Account Settings page.

After submitting the cancellation request:

• The account enters a 30-day recovery period.
• Customers can reactivate their account during this period by contacting customer support.
• After 30 days, all personal account data is permanently deleted except information legally required for tax and compliance purposes.

Orders already shipped cannot be cancelled through account deletion.

10. Warranty
------------------------------------------------------------

Most electronic products include a one-year manufacturer warranty.

Warranty claims require:

• Original invoice
• Product serial number
• Inspection by the service team

Physical damage caused by improper handling is not covered.

11. Exchange Policy
------------------------------------------------------------

Products received in damaged or incorrect condition may be exchanged within 7 days.

Exchange requests require:

• Original packaging
• Invoice
